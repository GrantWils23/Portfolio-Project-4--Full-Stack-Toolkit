Our Policy

Failure to attend an individuals first appointment will not incur a penalty charge, after a second missed appointment then the customer will be charged for failing to show up providing the booking has not been cancelled 1 day before the attended appointment date.

We attain the right to reject bookings outside our are code if the work is too far away. We will notify you immediately if this happens to be the case.

Parking

Whenever one of our staff has to drive to the location of the attended appointment and there is either parking and congestion charge fees, we charge the customer according to the charges our staff have had to pay.
